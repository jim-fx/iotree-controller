URLS:

  • GET /beemovie

    - Sends the beemovie script as morse to the star

  • GET /message/:message

    - Converts the message and sends it to the star

  • GET /random

    - Sets all random parameters on the tree

  • GET /star?r&g&b

    - Sets the rgb on the star

  • GET /light?r&g&b

    - Sets the rgb on the lights

  • GET /bg/:state

    - State = "on" or "off", turns the bg light on or off
